CyberTime Finance is a DeFi ecosystem. We create:

  • - Crypto Art Fantasy League ($ CAFL)
  • - Exclusive NFTs Collection
  • - Accelerator for talent development and new product launches

The Crypto Art Fantasy League ($ CAFL) is the world's first Art Fantasy League. Choose your favorite crypto artists, paintings and follow the voting. In the end, win valuable prizes!

Crypto Artists participate in monthly competitions. Income is formed from paid voting - 95% goes to the author and sales at 24-hour auctions.

Based on the created platform, we plan to create and sell the FLaS (Fantasy League as a Service) platform, where everyone can create their own fantasy league

Unique collection of CyberTime, which consists of 86.400 NFT. Each minute is unique and belongs to a specific author. A collection that is created in front of the community. 1,440 unique paintings, each with its own style and charisma

Part of the income from all projects goes to the buyback of CFT management tokens. They form the CyberTime Foundation. Within the framework of the fund, accelerators will be held to develop talents in the field of NFT, DeFi, Blockchain Games, Virtual Worlds etc. New projects will be created within the CyberTime ecosystem or independently, but most importantly, part of the income will go towards the buyback of CFT.
