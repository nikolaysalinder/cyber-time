We are creating the first NFT League Art and a platform to launch Fantasy League on the blockchain. Season 1 starts in 2021. There will be 8-12 stages and a grand final at the end of the year. Cryptoartists submit their works before each stage and the communities within 2 weeks vote for their favorite works with the native NFTL token. Before the start of voting, a Draft window opens, where a participant can create his Fantasy Team from cryptoartists and works presented at a particular stage. At the end of the voting, a 24 hour auction is held to sell the work you like.

To support the community, we have reserved 50% of NFTL tokens as rewards, which will be distributed over the next few seasons.
